# Feedback

Feedback and bug reports are managed through the NoLimitConnect issue tracker:

**[https://gitlab.com/nolimitcode/nolimitconnect/-/boards](https://gitlab.com/nolimitcode/nolimitconnect/-/boards)**

---

## Before Submitting

To help resolve your issue quickly, please include the following in your report:

### 1. Device Info

Open the **About No Limit Connect** page inside the application and copy the device information displayed there. Paste it into your issue report.

### 2. Description of the Issue

Provide a clear and detailed description of what is happening and what you expected to happen.

### 3. Steps to Reproduce

List the exact steps needed to reproduce the issue, for example:

1. Open the application
2. Navigate to ...
3. Perform action ...
4. Observe the result

Reports without reproduction steps may be difficult to investigate and may be closed.

---

## Submitting Your Report

1. Go to the issue tracker: [https://gitlab.com/nolimitcode/nolimitconnect/-/boards](https://gitlab.com/nolimitcode/nolimitconnect/-/boards)
2. Click **New Issue**
3. Include your device info, issue description, and reproduction steps
4. Submit the issue

!!! note
    A GitLab account is required to submit an issue. GitLab accounts are free.
